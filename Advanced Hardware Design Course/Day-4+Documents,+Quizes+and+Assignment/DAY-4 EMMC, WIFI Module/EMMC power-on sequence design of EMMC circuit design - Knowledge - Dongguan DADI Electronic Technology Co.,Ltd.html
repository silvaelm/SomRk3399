EMMC power-on sequence design of EMMC circuit design
- Sep 26, 2020-

1: Power supply sequence


The power supply of EMMC has two modes, and it works in two ways, VCC and VccQ. In the specification, the power-on sequence is required, as shown in the figure below.

EMMC power-on sequence


When powering up, VCC or VccQ can be the first to ramp up, or both can rise at the same time; at the same time, the power-on time of each power supply voltage should be less than the specified time tPRU (tPRUH, tPRUL or tPRUV). High voltage multimedia card: the maximum value of tPRU is 35mS, dual voltage multimedia card: the maximum value of tPRUL is 25mS, and the maximum value of tPRUH is 35mS.


In the design of the circuit, a suitable filter capacitor should be used to buffer the peak current. For the power supply filter capacitor, the parallel connection of large and small capacitors should be used, and the value of the large capacitor should not be less than 2.2uF. In order to better reduce the noise of the power supply, filter components such as magnetic beads are connected in series in the main circuit of the power supply.

Two: Bus signal line load capacitance and pull-up resistance


The total capacitance CL of each line of the EMMC bus is the sum of the bus master capacitance CHOST, the bus capacitance CBUS itself, and the capacitance CCARD of this line connected to the card.


CL = CHOST + CBUS + CCARD


And it is required that the sum of the host and bus capacitance does not exceed 20 pF.

1.2V and 1.8V power interface, the recommended maximum pull-up 50Kohm. With 3V power supply, the full range can be used up to 100Kohms.


The recommended CREG value and e•MMC equipment suppliers may vary. It is necessary to confirm the maximum value and the capacitance accuracy of the e•MMC manufacturer, because the electrical characteristics of the regulator in the e•MMC are affected by capacitance fluctuations.


Three: Schematic design of the specific circuit


For the circuit design of the memory, the main consideration is the integrity of the bus signal. A bad circuit may cause reflection, crosstalk, track collapse, and EMI problems. Therefore, in the circuit schematic design, it should be based on the specific parameters of the chip. Design the circuit with bus specifications, as long as the schematic design is reasonable, and then through reasonable PCB layout and wiring, the unstable factors of the system can be minimized.


3.1: According to the chip data, the VDDi pin of the chip needs an external capacitor. The value of this capacitor is limited, generally: min 0.1uF, max 1uF.


3.2: The filtering of the power circuit adopts the parallel connection of large and small capacitors, and at the same time connects the magnetic beads and other filter components in the main circuit to ensure the quality of the power signal. The value of the large capacitor should be greater than 2.2uF, and the small capacitor can be about 0.1uF.


3.3: Because it is a bus operation, in the design of the circuit, the state of the signal on the bus must be considered. Although e.MMC has internal pull-up resistors, once the data starts to transmit, these internal pull-up resistors will be automatically disconnected, so An external pull-up resistor is required to ensure that the signal level is fixed in sleep mode and will not appear in a floating state. The size of the pull-up resistor has a certain range, and it will be required according to the working voltage mode. For the pull-up resistors of DAT0-DAT7 and the reset terminal, use a resistance of about 50kΩ. Considering the price, a 51K resistor is generally used. It can meet the power supply requirements of 1.7-1.95V and also meet the power supply requirements of 2.7-3.6V; for the command line, a pull-up resistor of about 10KΩ is used, because the EMMC read and write operations are initiated by commands, it should have a relatively large The driving ability.


3.4: After testing, it is found that in the entire circuit of the bus operation, the signal on each data line has a certain overshoot and undershoot, which seriously affects the integrity of the signal and causes data transmission errors. This is a big reason because the impedance of the circuit is not matched. After testing, after a certain impedance matching, the signal's overshoot and undershoot are significantly reduced.


3.5: Use series resistance to achieve impedance matching. For the method of series resistance, firstly it plays the role of impedance matching, because the impedance of the signal source is very low and does not match the impedance of the signal line. After a resistor is connected in series, the matching situation can be improved. , In order to reduce reflections, avoid oscillation, etc.; at the same time, due to the high frequency of signal communication, a lot of high-frequency noise will be introduced. The series resistance will form an RC circuit with the distributed capacitance of the signal line and the input capacitance of the load, which will reduce the signal The steepness of the edge has a certain filtering effect on the signal and reducing noise.


3.6: The selection of the series resistance size needs to be determined according to the specific information provided by the chip. Generally, the series resistance on the bus is not very large. For example, Samsung's recommended value is 0-47Ω, and 27Ω is selected. Therefore, on all bus signal lines, we can connect a small resistor in series with each signal line. For the placement of resistors, the source of the clock should be matched, and for bidirectional data lines, the source and the terminal should be connected in series in theory, but considering the actual application of the circuit and the number of devices used, the terminal is generally matched.
